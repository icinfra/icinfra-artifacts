About

This site focuses on IT technology research and sharing.

The blog owner previously worked at HiSilicon, managing global IT/CAD infrastructure; and held an IT/CAD-related position in Tencent’s IC design and verification team, with a deep focus on IT architecture and the application and optimization of cutting-edge technologies. With extensive practical experience, they are dedicated to exploring automation and efficient work methods, actively seeking to leverage AI to enhance daily work efficiency.

Currently serving as the President of the Greater Bay Area Branch of the China CAD Alliance, they are committed to creating a platform for technical exchange, promoting industry prosperity, and progress. The China CAD Alliance aims to bridge the gap between solution providers and customers, fostering mutual development between technology and industry.

Feel free to visit https://icinfra.cn and engage with us on WeChat for further discussions. Some blog posts are also published on the CSDN homepage (https://blog.csdn.net/thesre), please stay tuned.
